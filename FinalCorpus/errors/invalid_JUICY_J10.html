About “2018 Grammys Performance”

Kendrick has been known to shake up an award show or two in his day, and this highly anticipated 2018 Grammy Awards performance was no exception.

Viewers were treated to another fearless medley with cameos from U2 and Dave Chappelle—plus a small army of dancers and a taiko drum to boot.

Kendrick took home the awards for Best Rap Album, Music Video, Rap Song, Rap Performance, and Best Rap/Sung Performance (with Rihanna).
